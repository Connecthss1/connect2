About Connect HSS

We empower Minnesotans with disabilities and seniors to find and maintain stable housing through comprehensive Housing Stabilization Services. Our dedicated team provides person-centered consultation services, guiding individuals toward secure, sustainable housing solutions that transform lives.

Our Services

  • Housing Consultation Build a comprehensive, person-centered housing plan tailored to your unique needs and goals.
  • Housing Transition Expert guidance in locating, securing, and transitioning to safe, affordable housing.
  • Housing Sustaining Ongoing support and resources to help you maintain stable housing long-term.

Eligibility Criteria

Housing Stabilization Services are available to Minnesota residents who meet these criteria:

Age Requirements Adults 18+ with disabilities or seniors 65+ are eligible for our services.
Medical Assistance Must be enrolled in Minnesota Medical Assistance (Medicaid) program.
Housing Status Currently homeless, at risk of homelessness, or living in institutional settings.
Assessment Complete a person-centered assessment to determine your specific service needs.
Provider Network Services must be provided through an enrolled HSS provider in Minnesota.

Contact us today for a free consultation to determine your eligibility and discuss your housing goals.
